Game Rules

Everything you need to know about playing FootballFusion

Registration

To play FootballFusion, you must first create an account. Registration is free and requires a valid email address, mobile number, and basic personal information.

Age Restrictions

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in FootballFusion, especially for contests involving real money prizes.

Account Verification

For security purposes and to comply with regulations, we require all users to verify their identity through a simple KYC process before withdrawing any winnings.

Pro Tip

Keep your account information up to date to avoid any issues with withdrawals or communications.

Budget

Each manager starts with a virtual budget of 100,000 points to build their team.

Squad Size

Your squad must consist of 15 players:

  • 2 Goalkeepers
  • 5 Defenders
  • 5 Midfielders
  • 3 Forwards

Team Composition

For each match day, you must select 11 players from your squad to form your starting lineup. The remaining 4 players will be on your bench.

Captain Selection

You must designate one player as your captain and another as your vice-captain. Your captain earns double points, and if your captain doesn't play, your vice-captain becomes your captain.

Pro Tip

Choose your captain wisely! A good captain choice can make a huge difference to your weekly score.

Pro Tip

Weekly Transfers

You are allowed one free transfer per game week. Any additional transfers will cost you 4 points per transfer.

Wildcards

You are given two wildcards per season - one to use in the first half of the season and one for the second half. A wildcard allows you to make unlimited transfers in a single game week without any point deductions.

Free Hit

The Free Hit chip lets you make unlimited free transfers for a single game week. At the next deadline, your squad will revert to how it was before you played the chip.

Bench Boost

The Bench Boost chip allows you to earn points from your bench players for one game week.

Triple Captain

The Triple Captain chip triples (instead of doubles) your captain's points for one game week.

üí°

Pro Tip

Save your chips for double gameweeks when teams play twice, maximizing your potential points!

Global League

All registered users automatically participate in the Global League, competing against all other players on the platform.

Private Leagues

You can create or join private leagues to compete against friends, family, or colleagues. Each private league has a unique code that you can share with others to invite them.

Weekly Contests

We host various weekly contests with different entry fees and prize pools. These contests are based on the performance of your team for that specific game week only.

Season-Long Contests

Season-long contests run throughout the entire football season, with prizes awarded based on your cumulative points at the end of the season.

üí°

Pro Tip

Create a private league with your friends to add an extra competitive edge to your fantasy experience!

Cash Prizes

Cash prizes are awarded to top performers in various contests. The prize distribution structure is clearly mentioned before you enter any contest.

Withdrawals

You can withdraw your winnings through various payment methods including UPI, bank transfers, and popular e-wallets. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours.

Taxes

As per Indian regulations, TDS will be deducted on winnings exceeding 10,000 points. You are responsible for declaring your winnings and paying any applicable taxes.

üí°

Pro Tip

Keep your KYC documents updated to ensure smooth and quick withdrawal of your winnings!

Multiple Accounts

Creating multiple accounts is strictly prohibited. Each user is allowed only one account on FootballFusion.

Collusion

Any form of collusion or match-fixing between users is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension.

Suspicious Activity

We monitor all accounts for suspicious activity. Any account found engaging in fraudulent activities will be suspended, and winnings may be forfeited.

Penalties

Violation of our fair play policy may result in warnings, point deductions, contest disqualification, account suspension, or permanent banning, depending on the severity of the violation.
