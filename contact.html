Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create multiple teams for the same match?

+

Yes! You can create up to 3 different team combinations for the same match. This gives you more chances to win and lets you try different strategies with various player combinations.

What happens if a match gets cancelled or postponed?

+

If a match is cancelled or postponed, all contests related to that match will be automatically voided and entry fees will be refunded to your account within 24 hours. Your team selections will remain saved for when the match is rescheduled.

Do substitutes earn points for my team?

+

Yes, if your selected player is substituted during a match, you'll earn points for both the starting player and their substitute proportional to their playing time. This makes team selection even more strategic!

Can I join private leagues from different time zones?

+

Absolutely! Our platform automatically adjusts all deadlines and match times to your local time zone. You can compete with friends from anywhere in the world without worrying about time differences or missing team submission deadlines.
